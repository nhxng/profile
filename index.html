My favorite dishes

Bun Rieu

Bún riêu is a traditional Vietnamese meat rice vermicelli soup. Bún riêu cua is served with tomato broth and topped with freshwater crab.

Kem Xoi

Kem xôi is sweet Vietnamese dish made from sticky rice combined with ice cream, my favorite toppings are coconut ice cream and roasted peanuts.

Pho

Phở is another traditional Vietnamese soup, typically made from beef stock and herbs to which noodles and thinly sliced beef or chicken are added.
